UNIVERSITY BOXING

Forged in the heart of Texas A&M, our University Boxing program focuses on raw training, student inclusion, and the relentless development of discipline. Whether you are stepping into the ring for the first time or honing years of experience, we provide the crucible to build mental and physical fortitude.

FARMER'S FIGHT NIGHT

More than a competition; it is a commitment to our community. Farmer's Fight Night is our premier charity exhibition, bringing together the Aggie spirit for a night of intense matchups and profound impact. Witness the dedication of our fighters as they battle for a cause greater than themselves.

NATIONAL CHAMPIONS

Excellence is not an aspiration; it is our standard. Our USIBA team represents the pinnacle of collegiate boxing. We emphasize elite performance, competitive rigor, and a winning mindset, consistently proving our dominance on the national stage.
